Brad Myers's Advisee Tree

At the ACM CHI 2017 conference, Brad Myers was awarded the CHI Lifetime Research Award.
In his keynote talk, he presented his advisee tree — all 6 generations, totaling 176 students!

The original hierarchical document with affiliations, and the visualized data, are available.
